Mehmet Kaya

PROFESSOR AT DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER ENGINEERING

Mehmet KAYA received his BSc, MSc and PhD degrees in Electrical-Electronics Engineering and Computer Engineering from Firat University, Turkey, in 1996, 1998 and 2003, respectively. Currently, he is Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering, Firat University. He spent 2002 as a Visiting Scholar at the ADSA Laboratory, Department of Computer Science, University of Calgary, Canada. He has published over 90 papers in refereed international journals and conferences. His primary work and research interests are in the areas of data mining, social network analysis and machine learning.
